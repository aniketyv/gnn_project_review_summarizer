  =========================================================================
  
  
  ROUGE = Recall-Oriented Understudy for Gisting Evaluation. 
  It measures how much overlap exists between your generated summary 
  and a reference summary.
  
  
  Reference:  "the food was great and the service was friendly"
Generated:  "the food is great and staff were friendly"

ROUGE-1: overlap of single words
  Matching words: the, food, great, and, friendly = 5
  Score = 5 / 8 = 0.625

ROUGE-2: overlap of word pairs (bigrams)
  Matching pairs: "food was/is" partial, "and friendly" = 1
  Score = 1 / 7 = 0.143

ROUGE-L: longest common subsequence
  Looks for longest matching sequence in order
  "the food ... great and ... friendly" = 0.571
